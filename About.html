So, who am i?

2002 I was born in a small town in the southwest part of Macedonia
2007 Had my first touch with a computer
2008 Started Elementary School
2012 Learned some basic computer skills
2017 Started High School in my hometown
2018 I fell in love with maths and programming
2019 Got involved in a lot of math competitions and science projects
2020 Got my first idea of what cyber security is
2021 Started University at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering in Skopje

I am Jovan Simonoski, a 20 years old IT student form Resen, Macedonia. Born on 18th of January, I was a quiet kid who always enjoyed playing on his computer and exploring new things. I learned my first basic computer skills while trying to download different games and programs and exploring them as well. I also had a lot of friends who were enthusiastically using their computers, and we always shared the knowledge we gained among us.

Once I was 6 years old, it was time to go the Elementary School and I was very excited. The school was called "Mite Bogoevski" and was located in the center of my hometown. There I had my first idea of how important science is for our everyday life, especially I loved maths and was good at it. I had many opportunities to participate in maths, chemistry and biology competitions. I always loved to make new friends and to be in a space surrounded with technology and people working with it.

When I finished Elementary School, I was pretty sure that I wanted to study a lot of maths and science in general, so I decided the High School in my hometown, SOU "Car Samoil" was the right choice. There I met some of the most impressing people of my entire life. Some professors changed my life forever. I was part of a lot of projects and organizations connected with the school. We even formed a youth club called "Prespa Youth Club" and we organized few events like marathons, concerts etc. In those days I fell deeply in love with music and got into my first Rock N' Roll band called "Instinct". Since then, we played a lot of gigs and concerts. We even recorded our own songs, and we had a wonderful experience in general. Those are some moments I'll never forget.

After 4 years of constantly learning and developing myself, it was time for University, and when it comes to that, I can say that I couldn't make a better choice than FINKI. The amount of happiness and joy I felt when I started cannot be described with words. I finally found myself and the environment I want to grow in as a person and as a professional.
